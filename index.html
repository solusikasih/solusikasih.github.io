Who is Solusi Kasih?

Based in Bangi, Solusi Kasih Sdn Bhd is a dedicated provider of caregiving training and home-based support. We bridge the gap in healthcare by equipping caregivers with the competence to provide high-quality care with a heart of compassion.

What is Palliative Care?

Palliative care is specialized medical care for people living with serious illnesses. It focuses on providing relief from symptoms and improving quality of life for both the patient and the family.
